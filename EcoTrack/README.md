# EcoTrack: Carbon Footprint Calculator

## Overview
EcoTrack tracks your carbon footprint, and lets you know of your progress. It gives you suggestions to be more sustainable, and shows analytics based on your carbon footprint and its composition.


## Features
- Survey that asks for basic information related to carbon footprint output.
- Results screen with sustainability score and tree animation (volume of apples depends on sustainability score)
- Charts screen with composition of carbon footprint, comparison with world averages and UAE averages, and monthly progress.
- Printable charts screen.
- World Bank APIs get real information for world averages and country averages.
